About the Library


Matrix web-based library management system engages users by providing self-service functionality and encouraging collaboration. It reduces the pressure on library staff and increases access to knowledge from any device including mobile smart phones and tablets.


Members can register themselves and gain access to this library portal at anytime, from anywhere. They gain the facility of e-Library-Browsing, which means browsing and reserving books via the internet.
